01

DISCOVERY

The initial phase of my design process involves engaging in a detailed discussion with the client to thoroughly understand their objectives, target audience, and specific requirements. This step ensures that the project is in line with their vision and effectively supports their business goals.

02

MOODBOARDING

I do an analysis of the client's industry, competitors, and target demographic to guide my design strategy. Additionally, I develop a sitemap and wireframes using Relume to meticulously outline the website's architecture and layout.

03

VISUAL DESIGN

Once the planning is done, I move on to designing the visual elements of the website, such as the color scheme, typography, and imagery. I create a high-fidelity mockup in Figma that represents the final design.

04

DEVELOPMENT

With the design finalized, I begin the development process using tools like Webflow or Framer to create responsive websites. I also incorporate GSAP for animations and Jitter for dynamic video content.

WHAT'S MY

PROCESS LIKE?

My design process is collaborative, involving close communication with clients to ensure their vision is realized. I prioritize understanding their needs and goals, which allows me to tailor each project to their specific requirements.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

QUESTIONS?

Here are some answers to frequently asked questions about my design process, services, and areas of expertise.

Timeline

What is the typical duration for completing a project?

Project timelines vary based on the scope and complexity. Smaller projects, such as landing pages or branding work, usually take 2–4 weeks. In contrast, larger projects, like full website design and development, can take 6–8 weeks or more. After discussing the details, I'll provide a more precise timeline.

Budget

What are the typical costs associated with my project?

The cost of a project is determined by its size and specific requirements. Branding projects begin at $1500, while website design and Webflow development typically range from $5,000 to $12,000. To get a tailored quote, let's discuss your unique needs and objectives.

Process

Can you describe the step-by-step process from initiation to completion?

Each project begins with a discovery phase to understand your goals, audience, and vision. I then develop initial concepts and refine them based on your feedback. If Webflow development is part of the project, I convert the designs into a functional, responsive site. The final phase involves testing, making necessary revisions, and ensuring a smooth handoff, so everything is polished and ready for launch.
